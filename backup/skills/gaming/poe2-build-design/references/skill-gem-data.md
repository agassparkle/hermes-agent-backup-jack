# PoE2 Skill Gem Data (extracted from wiki, June 2026 — v0.5.x)

Key skills with damage numbers, costs, and mechanics. Organized by tier and synergy potential.

## Power Charge Engine (Monk / Quarterstaff)

### Killing Palm (Tier 1)
- 90–281% attack damage, 6–42 mana, 75% base AS
- Culling Strike, Can't be Evaded
- Dash to cullable targets, strikes additional cullable targets in range
- Grants Power Charges: 1 (normal), 2 (rare), 3 (unique)
- Quality: Recover 4% max mana over 4s on cull

### Falling Thunder (Tier 1)
- 140–437% attack damage, 11–76 mana, 60% base AS
- 60% phys → lightning, Cone 4m
- **Consumes all Power Charges** to fire Lightning Projectiles forward
- Quality: +20% more damage per Power Charge consumed

### Siphoning Strike (Tier 7)
- 75–234% strike damage, 7–47 mana
- **Consumes Shock** on target → releases 200–624% shockwave + grants Power Charge
- Dash, +6 melee range, 50% more stun buildup
- Shockwave: 100% phys → lightning, Cannot Shock, 3m radius

### Storm Wave (Tier 7)
- 110–343% attack damage, 8–54 mana, 75% base AS
- 80% phys → lightning, 100% more Shock chance
- Wave 8m length, 1m width

### Ice Strike (Tier 5)
- 60–187% attack damage, 5–36 mana, 140% base AS
- 80% phys → cold, 3-hit combo with powerful finisher
- Quality: 0–20% inc Attack Speed (was 0–10% more — nerfed in 0.5.0)

### Tempest Flurry (Tier 5)
- 45–140% normal strikes, 135–421% 4th strike, 5–37 mana, 140% base AS
- 60% phys → lightning (normal), 100% conversion (4th strike)
- 4th strike: 100% more Shock chance, 100% more Shock magnitude, +6 range

### Glacial Cascade (Tier 1)
- 40–125% per burst, 9–63 mana, 75% base AS
- 80% phys → cold, 8 bursts in a sequence
- Consumes Freeze on frozen enemies for heavy final-spike damage

### Boneshatter (Tier 1)
- 100–312% initial strike
- Shockwave: 250–780% when enemy is Primed for Stun
- Cannot cause Stun buildup (initial), 2m shockwave radius

### Vaulting Impact (Tier 5)
- 160–422% attack damage, 12–83 mana
- Leap + Slam, shockwave applies Broken Stance to Dazed enemies

### Frozen Locus (Tier 1)
- 12–81 mana, 70% base AS
- Creates Ice Crystal (27–9536 max life), 10s duration, limit 1

## Notable Synergies to Investigate

1. **Killing Palm → Flicker Strike**: Kill cullable enemies → 1-3 Power Charges per kill → Flicker consumes for 2×charges additional strikes at 285% AS. At 6 charges: 13 strikes × 327% = 4,251% base. MUST generate charges between Flicker casts (lockout).
2. **Killing Palm → Falling Thunder**: Power Charge cycle (generator → spender), simpler rotation, 437% × 2.2 = 961% at 6 charges.
3. **Storm Wave → Siphoning Strike → Flicker Strike**: Shock (343%, 100% more) → consume for 624% shockwave + Power Charge → Flicker Strike nuclear payoff.
4. **Volcano + Shockwave Totem**: Totem Slams trigger Volcano sprays
5. **Thunderstorm + Lightning Rod + Lightning Arrow**: Drench → beams → rod charges
6. **Frostbolt + Ice Nova + Snap**: Projectile delivery + Nova + ailment consumption
7. **Whirling Slash → Twister**: Whirlwind consumption for 80% more damage per stage
8. **Rapid Assault → Blood Hunt**: Bleed stacking → consumed for 500% more AoE explosion
9. **Contagion + Profane Ritual + Detonate Dead**: Chaos spread + corpse consumption engine
10. **Gathering Storm + Tempest Bell**: Perfect dash detonates bell for 564–869% attack damage (0.5.0 buff)
