# PoE2 v0.5.0 Key Changes (for build design)

Patch 0.5.0 — major overhaul. Sub-patches 0.5.1–0.5.3 were minor bugfixes.

## Defense Meta Shift

### ES Gutted
- ES Recharge Rate mods cut ~65%: "of Suffusion" 20-23% (was 46-50%), "of Ardour" 16-19% (was 41-45%), "of Buffering" 12-15% (was 36-40%).
- Faster start of ES Recharge small passives: 6% (was 15%).
- ES Recharge Rate small passives REMOVED from tree, replaced by lower-value Faster Start nodes.
- Essence of Hysteria on Foci: 20-23% ES recharge (was 41-45%).
- **Verdict**: Pure ES builds are dead. Hybrid defense mandatory.

### Ghost Dance Reworked
- No longer duration-based. Now CDR-based (Modifiers to Cooldown Recovery Rate apply).
- Gains Ghost Shroud every 11.7–10.1s at lv4–20 (was 7.6–6.1).
- "When Hit, lose a Ghost Shroud"
- "Regenerate Energy Shield equal to 2% of your Evasion Rating per second if you have lost a Ghost Shroud Recently"
- **Synergy**: Evasion gear + Ghost Dance = ES recovery that bypasses recharge nerf.

### Barkskin Buffed
- Now provides 50% ES lost as Armour at lv8 (was 32%), scaling to 62% at lv20 (was 44%).

### New Hybrid Clusters
- Deflection + Faster Start of ES Recharge clusters added to passive tree.
- Small passives: Deflection = 8% of Evasion (was 6%).

### Armour/Evasion Notable Nerfs
- Iron Slippers: 2 Armour per 1 ES on boots (was 3).
- Subterfuge Mask: 1 Evasion per 1 ES on helmet (was 2).
- Wild Cat: 12% Evasion as Deflection (was 10%).

## New Ascendancies

### Martial Artist (Monk)
- "Master of illusions. Empowered by runes socketed into his body, creates powerful illusions, summons illusory bells, excels at using hands as weapons."
- Ascendancy node details NOT yet on wiki (brand new in 0.5.0).

### Spirit Walker (Huntress)
- "Bound to the wilds. Calls upon spirits — Stag, Owl, or Bear. Subdues formidable beasts, binds them in spirit, calls forth spectral companion."
- Ascendancy node details NOT yet on wiki.

## Ascendancy Changes

### Chronomancer
- **Phased Form** (new notable): Take 30% less Damage. After 4s of being hit, take damage equal to 30% of prevented damage.
- **Now and Again** (reworked): Cascadable Spells 20% chance to Echo. Repeatable Spells 20% chance to Repeat.
- **Temporal Rift**: Cast time 0.1s (was 0.5s). Now also removes delayed damage from Phased Form.
- **Sands of Time**: 1-60% increased Skill Speed (was Cast Speed only).
- **Unbound Encore**: Repositioned in tree.

### Stormweaver
- Unchanged from 0.4.0.

### Blood Mage
- **Sanguine Tides**: Gain 1 Life Flask Charge per 2% Life spent (was per 4%). Gain 1% damage as extra phys per charge consumed for 5s (was 3s). Flasks do not recover Life (was 50% less).
- **Vitality Siphon**: 20% spell damage leeched as life (was 10%).

### Gemling Legionnaire
- **Advanced Thaumaturgy**: reworked — Gem Quality now grants socketed skills an additional effect.

## Skill Changes

### Buffed
- **Earthquake**: Aftershock 184-666% attack damage (was 160-580%).
- **Cull the Weak**: 109-281% attack dmg (was 60-156%). Can't be Evaded. AS 75% base (was 60%).
- **Tame Beast**: Summoned Beasts deal 40-84% more damage at lv9-20. Immediate summon if spirit available.
- **Gathering Storm**: Perfect Dash detonates Tempest Bell for 564-869% attack damage. Shocked Ground left behind.
- **Barkskin**: 50-62% ES lost as armour (was 32-44%).
- **Banners** (Defiance/Dread/War): No movement penalty. Base radius 6m (was 4.5m).
- **Unborn Lich staff skills**: All now 0 mana cost. Vile Intrusion: 100% inc Impale (was 2-40%), 2-3 impales (was 1). Winnowing Flame: 30% ignite consumption (was 25%).
- **Eye of Winter**: Projectile count now modifiable by +projectiles.
- **Crossbow Ammo skills**: Base cost now 0 (was fully disabled cost), can gain additional costs.

### Nerfed
- **Comet**: 787-1181 Cold at lv20 (was 829-1243). Fire-Infused also nerfed proportionally.
- **Freezing Salvo**: 34-68% more chill/freeze (was 56-107%).
- **Lightning Arrow**: Multiple beams can't chain to same target.
- **Ice Shot**: Lost 25% more Freeze Buildup.
- **Ice Strike**: Quality now 0-20% inc Attack Speed (was 0-10% more Attack Speed).
- **Magma Barrier**: 5-7 fire dmg per 15 armour (was 6-8).
- **Boneshatter**: Quality 0-20% inc AS (was 0-30%).
- **Ghost Dance**: Shroud interval 10.1-11.7s (was 6.1-7.6s).

### Reworked
- **Ice Nova**: Can no longer originate from Frostbolt while cascading sideways.
- **Spell Cascade**: No longer limited to spells you use yourself.
- **Feral Invocation**: No longer bypasses cooldown of socketed skills.
- **Command Skills**: 50% movement penalty (was 70%).
- **Ancestral Warrior Totem**: Hidden 0.6s delay removed, replaced with 50% of attack time.
- **Ancestral Bond Keystone**: Totem limit doubled, no charge requirement, totems reserve 75 Spirit each.

## Item Changes

### Uniques — Unchanged/Building-Enabling
- **Indigon**: UNCHANGED. Still (10-15)% spell dmg per 200 mana spent, (5-10)% cost increase.
- **Cloak of Defiance**: UNCHANGED. 50% MoM.

### Uniques — Buffed
- **Inspiration Runes**: 20/25/30% mana regen in wands/staves (was 16/20/24%).
- **Mind Runes**: +20/30/40 max mana in armour (was +15/25/35).
- **Rebirth Runes**: 0.35/0.4/0.45% life regen in armour (was 0.25/0.3/0.35%).
- **Collapsing Horizon**: 100% inc Elemental Damage (was Elemental Damage with Attacks).
- **Svalinn**: 200-300% inc Armour (was 150-200%).
- **Keeper of the Arc**: 40% less damage with hits/DoT (was 30%).
- **Solus Ipse**: 2 additional Lineage Support copies (was 1).
- **Morior Invictus**: Values per socket filled roughly doubled across all mods.
- **Ancestral Bond**: totems reserve 75 Spirit each, totem limit doubled, no charge req.

### Uniques — Nerfed
- **The Covenant**: 10-25% life cost efficiency (was 25-50%).
- **Hyrri's Ire**: Changed from flat cold to attacks → "Gain 15-25% damage as extra cold". Lost flat cold.
- **Whispering Ice**: 2% spell dmg per 10 Int (was 4%). Exposure 50-60% (was 20-30%) — net mixed.
- **Perfidy**: 10-30% avoid chaos (was 10-40%). Now also avoid fire/cold/lightning separately.

### Crafting
- +Gem Level on weapons nerfed: Tier 1 two-hand +5 (was +7). One-hand +4 (was +5). Quiver +projectile: +1 (was +2).
- "Lord's" Spirit mod: 20-26% inc Spirit (was 30-36%) — bugfix.
- Cast Speed mods on rings/amulets separated from foci/wands (can now roll on all).

## Meta Implications

1. **Indigon mana-stacker still strong** — survived untouched, MoM relatively better post-ES-nerf.
2. **Evasion + Ghost Dance + ES hybrid** is the new caster defense meta.
3. **Martial Artist + Spirit Walker** open new build space — nodes undocumented, potential for discovery.
4. **Tame Beast** (40-84% more) + Minion Splash supports = new minion archetype.
5. **Weapon +levels nerfed** makes unique weapons with inherent +levels more valuable.
6. **Chronomancer's Phased Form (30% less dmg)** is game-changing for tanky caster builds.
